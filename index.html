North American Shared Retention Commitments

In 2012, Sustainable Collection Services and the Michican Shared Print Initiative undertook one of the first shared print monographs projects in the US.

Since then, ten other groups have quietly continued this important work with the help of SCS (part of OCLC since 2015) and the GreenGlass Model Builder.

The results speak for themselves: over 145 academic institutions spanning fourteen U.S. states and five Canadian provinces have joined the OCLC Share Print Initiative. Those institutions have identified 7.6 million distinct editions for long-term retention, representing 19.7 million print holdings across North America. Share print initiatives are also starting to take hold across the world, with programs in the United Kingdom and Australia.
