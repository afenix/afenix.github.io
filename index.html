North American Shared Retention Commitments

Eleven Group Projects now exist in North America with membership of over 145 academic institutions spanning fourteen U.S. states and five Canadian provinces.

Through a collaborative process of shared print strategies these institutions have been able to retain print collections of 19.7 million title-holdings, with 7.6 million of those titles retained being distinct editions.
